## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is there a trial period?**
A: Yes, 30-day evaluation with all Enterprise features. No credit card required.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Wire transfer, ACH, credit card, Purchase Order (government/agencies).

**Q: Does pricing include support?**
A: All tiers include support. See comparison matrix for levels.

**Q: Can I upgrade tiers later?**
A: Yes, pay the difference between tiers. Prorated for the remaining license period.

**Q: Is there a perpetual license?**
A: Yes, one-time purchase includes perpetual use. Optional annual maintenance (20% of license) for updates.

**Q: Do you offer discounts for non-profits?**
A: Yes, 40% discount for registered non-profits and educational institutions.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my limits?**
A: Soft limits with warnings. Hard limits require upgrade or additional purchase.
